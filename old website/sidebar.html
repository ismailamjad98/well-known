Colarus Group is proud to sign an agency agreement with Xuzhou Industrial Fabrics China as the sole agent/importer in North America ( Canada, USA and Mexico)..

Presently, it is major supplier to Chinese pulp and paper Industry, and is exporting to South America, Far East, South Asia and Easter Europe.

  • Papermaking Felt Fabric
