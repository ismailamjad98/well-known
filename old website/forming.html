Forming Fabric Papermaking Felt Fabric Dryer Fabric
Introduction:

Forming fabric wire is a filtration medium and works like the heart of paper machine. The function of forming fabric wire is to weave the fibers and dewater the stock so that paper web formed is sufficiently strong to run through the presses and dries up to end product.

According to the weaving, forming fabric can be divided into 4-shed, 5-shed, 7-shed, 8-shed, 16-shed, 24-shed etc. According to the category, it can be divided into single layer fabric, double layer fabric, two-and a half layer fabric and three-layer fabric.

Advantages:

  • Improved paper machine efficiencies
  • Enhanced sheet smoothness
  • Increased fiber support
  • Excellent stability
  • Low drag load
  • Low water carry
  • High fiber support
